About

This bot helps you resize and convert videos directly in Slack.

Simply upload a video to a channel where the bot is present, and it will offer options to:

  • Resize to common resolutions (720p, 1080p, 480p)
  • Convert horizontal videos to vertical format

How to Use

  1. Upload a video to Slack
  2. The bot will respond with options
  3. Select your desired action
  4. Wait for processing
  5. Receive the processed video
